Account & Data Deletion Request

App: The Lost Knight (developer: Adir Ofir) — com.adir.thelostknight

This page explains how to request deletion of your The Lost Knight account and all data associated with it.

What gets deleted: Your username, all cloud save data (HP, coins, potions, sword damage, game progress flags), and any record of your account on our backend services.

How long it takes: Up to 30 days from the date we receive your request.

How to Request Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, send an email to the address below containing the following information:

Email to: adirofir123@gmail.com

Subject line: Account Deletion Request - The Lost Knight

Required in the email body:

  1. Your in-game username (the name you use to log in to The Lost Knight)
  2. A short confirmation that you want this account and all associated data permanently deleted

What Happens Next

  1. You will receive a reply within 7 days acknowledging the request.
  2. Your account and all cloud save data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of the request being received.
  3. Once deleted, the data cannot be recovered. If you log in again later with the same username, you will start with a fresh save.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your chosen username
  • Game progress data, including:
    • Maximum health points (HP)
    • Coin count
    • Potion count
    • Sword damage value
    • Story progression flags (which events you have completed)
    • Ability unlock flags
  • Any internal identifiers associated with your account in Unity Gaming Services

Data That Is Not Retained

The Lost Knight does not collect or store the following information at any point, so there is nothing of this type to delete:

  • Your real name
  • Your email address (other than for the deletion request itself, which is also removed after the request is processed)
  • Your phone number
  • Your physical location
  • Any payment or financial information
  • Any photos, videos, files, or contacts from your device

Questions?

If you have any questions about this deletion process or our data practices in general, please review our Privacy Policy or contact us at adirofir123@gmail.com.

This deletion process applies to The Lost Knight (com.adir.thelostknight) available on the Google Play Store.
