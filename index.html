Privacy Policy for The Lost Knight

Last updated: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Adir Ofir ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use the mobile game The Lost Knight ("the Game") on Android devices.

By using The Lost Knight, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the Game:

Account Information

To enable cloud save features, the Game asks you to choose a username. This is the only piece of personal identifier we collect from you.

We do not ask for, collect, or store:

  • Your real name
  • Your email address
  • Your age or birthdate
  • Your phone number
  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Photos, videos, or files from your device
  • Any password you create yourself

Gameplay Data (Cloud Save)

To allow you to continue your progress across devices and sessions, we save the following gameplay data to the cloud, linked to your username:

  • Maximum health points (HP)
  • Coin count
  • Potion count
  • Sword damage value
  • Game progress flags (which story events you have completed, abilities you have unlocked, etc.)

Automatically Collected Information

We do not collect any analytics, advertising IDs, device identifiers, IP addresses, or similar tracking information beyond what is technically necessary to associate your gameplay data with your chosen username.

Backend Service

To provide the username-based cloud save functionality, the Game uses Unity Gaming Services, operated by Unity Technologies. When you create or log in to your in-game account, Unity Gaming Services may generate an internal identifier (Player ID) used to link your username to your save data on Unity's servers. This identifier is not shown to you and is used only to deliver the cloud save feature.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Provide the Game's core functionality
  • Save your game progress to the cloud so you can continue playing across devices
  • Let you resume your saved game by re-entering your username
  • Resolve technical issues you may report

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating the Game.

3. How Your Information Is Stored

All username and cloud save data is stored using Unity Gaming Services, a service provided by Unity Technologies. Unity Gaming Services securely stores this data on their servers.

You can review Unity's privacy practices here: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We share your information only with:

  • Unity Technologies, the provider of the backend services (authentication and cloud save) that the Game uses to function
  • Authorities or legal entities, if required by law, court order, or government regulation

5. Data Retention

We retain your username and cloud save data for as long as your account exists. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us using the email address provided at the bottom of this policy. We will delete your account and all associated cloud save data within 30 days of your request.

6. Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for general audiences and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Backend authentication and storage use industry-standard encryption provided by Unity Gaming Services. However, the Game's account system does not currently require a user-chosen password to log in, only a username. This means anyone who knows your username could potentially access your save data. We recommend choosing a unique username that is not your real name or otherwise easy to guess.

9. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party service:

  • Unity Gaming Services — for account authentication and cloud save functionality

The Game does not include:

  • Advertising networks
  • Third-party analytics services
  • Social media integrations
  • In-app purchases

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated through the Game or its Google Play Store listing.
